About Me

I am currently working as a software developer for SGWorld Ltd. I work as part of a small team who are focused on moving some of our older products into the current era, using cloud based solutions with a variety of different technologies such as .NET Core, Angular7, Microsoft SQL among others.

After graduating from Coventry University in Q3 of 2017 with a first class degree in Ethical Hacking, this has been my first full time job. I am extremely thankful for the experience I have been able to pick up whilst at SG World.

In my spare time, I tend to lean towards using NodeJS (usually with Typescript) or Python, both of which I enjoy for their simplicity, endless amounts of documentation, support and ease of use. Both of these languages have their quirks, but that usually adds to the fun.

Now I have gained some experience in the field, it is a goal of mine to start contributing to more open-source projects. I really enjoy the collaboration, purpose and culture surrounding open-source development and I realise that they are the basis for many of the tools, technologies and even commercial products we use in our day-to-day lives.

“It is the challenge to approach the world in an innovative way, looking for solutions that break from tradition, and doing so in a collaborative environment where transparency of process is the most important virtue.”  [Adam Monago, ThoughtWorks]

Research Assistant (Intership)
