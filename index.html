About Me

I am currently working as a software developer for SGWorld Ltd. I work as part of a small team who are focused on moving some of our older products into the current era, using cloud based solutions with a variety of different technologies.

Whilst here I have mostly been working with .NET Core, developing MVC applications, and a number of different Javascript frameworks for client-side applications, Angular, Electron and Nativescript to name a few. I have also been able to gain experience with a number of database engines including MongoDB, MicrosoftSQL, and PostgreSQL. I was also largely involved in our move to Azure and setup most of our devops pipelines for building, testing and deploying our application.

In my spare time,I tend to lean towards using NodeJS (usually with Typescript) or Python, both of which I enjoy for their simplicity, endless amounts of documentation, support and ease of use. Both of these languages have their quirks, bt that usually adds to the fun.

I graduated from Coventry University in Q3 of 2017 with a first class degree in Ethical Hacking and Network Security, besides the odd few months of work here and there this has been my first full time job. I am extremely thankful for the experience I have been able to pick up whilst at SGWorld.
